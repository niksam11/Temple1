Do's or Dont's

Do's

  • Do keep your mind fresh and leave all your negative, stress and pain behind.
  • Do open your eyes while having Darshan.
  • Do observe maintain silence inside the Temple.
  • Do respect ancient customs and usages while in temple premises.
  • Do respect and promote religious sentiments among co-pilgrims.
  • Do deposit your offerings & Kanukas in the Hundi Only.
  • Do keep the temple premises Clean & Hygienic.
  • Do contact information centre for unknown information about devasthanam.
  • Do watch your valuables very carefully (i.e Wallets, Gold Ornaments, Luggage bags etc.,) as there is fear of theft.

Dont's

  • Don't carry Jewellery and Cash in excess with you.
  • Don't wear footwear in and around the premises of the Temple.
  • Don't approach brokers for quick Darshan.
  • Don't throw away the Prasadam and teertham given to you at the temple.
  • Don't carry any weapon inside the temple.
  • Don't eat non-vegetarian food.
  • Don't consume liquor or other intoxicants.
  • Don't smoke in and around temple premises.
  • Don't wear any head guards like helmets, caps, turbans and hats inside the temple premises.
  • Don't buy fake Prasadams from street vendors.
  • Don't commit any act of violence or harshness, anger, Jealous in the temple premises.
  • Don't quarrel in the temple premises.
  • Don't rush in for Darshan but take your chance in the queue.
  • Don't encourage beggary in and around temple premises.
  • Don't spit or be a nuisance inside the temple premises.
  • Don't carry luggage bags and other belongs inside temple premises.
